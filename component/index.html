Frequently Asked Questions

What items can I ship internationally?

You can ship documents, clothes, electronics, sweets, spices, ayurvedic products, jewelry, and most non-hazardous items. We handle customs clearance and provide professional packing.

How much does shipping cost?

Costs depend on weight, dimensions, destination, and service type. Contact us for a free quote - we offer competitive rates with our courier partners FedEx, DHL, Aramex, and UPS.

Do you provide tracking?

Yes! All shipments include real-time tracking via WhatsApp, SMS, and email updates. You'll receive tracking numbers immediately after pickup.

Is my shipment insured?

Absolutely. All shipments are fully insured up to the declared value. We also provide professional packing and take photos of valuable items before shipping.

How do I get a quote?
