About FlyMyCart Courier

FlyMyCart Courier is a Bengaluru-based logistics and e-commerce platform dedicated to making international shipping and shopping for Indian products simple, fast, and trustworthy. Founded to serve NRIs, students, families and small businesses, we combine authentic Indian grocery shopping with powerful courier services to 240+ countries.

Whether you're sending sweets for Diwali, documents for university admission, clothes to family abroad, or product samples for your business — we handle everything with care. We partner with FedEx, DHL, Aramex and UPS to offer the best combination of speed, price and reliability.

Our online store brings India closer to you — masalas, snacks, puja items, ayurvedic products, sweets, dry fruits and more — delivered worldwide with professional packing and full tracking.

Located in Kalyan Nagar, Bengaluru, we offer same-day pickup across the city. Every shipment is insured, photographed if valuable, and tracked in real-time. Our support team is just a call or WhatsApp away — real people, real help.
